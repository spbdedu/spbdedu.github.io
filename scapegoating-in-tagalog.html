3. The situation when people encounter problem that they do not know how to solve it. A. Ethnocentrismb. Racismc. Stereotypingd. Scapegoating​.


Answer:

scapegoating

Explanation:

Scapegoating is a psychological and social phenomenon in which an individual or group is blamed for something that they did not do, often to deflect blame or responsibility away from the actual perpetrators. This can be a form of bullying or discrimination, as the scapegoat is often targeted unfairly and subjected to mistreatment or exclusion.
