10. Elaine is in the business of repairing home computers. She charges a base fee of 400 pesos and 200 pesos per hour for her labor. How many hours must be done for Elaine to make a profit of 1,400 pesos?​


ANSWER:

5 HOURS

Step-by-step explanation:

1,400(her profit) -400(base fee)= 1000 pesos she needed to earn

1 hour = 200 pesos

1,000 pesos / 200 pesos/hour

5 hour

Answer:

5 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

As function:

f(x) = 400 + 200x  

Where:

f(x) = profit

x = number of hours

Solve for number of hours:

1,400 = 400 + 200x

200x = 1,400 - 400

200x/200 = 1,000/200

x = 5 hours
