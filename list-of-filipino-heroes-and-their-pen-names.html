19. list of the heroes in the philippines?


Here are some heroes and their accomplishments:

Dr. Jose P. Rizal (1861-1896) The Philippines' national hero.  He contributed various literary works to La Solidaridad. For his leadership in the reform movement and for his incendiary novels,Andres Bonifacio  (1863-1897) He founded the secret society, Katipunan and  He was also president of the Tagalog republic Melchora Aquino  (1812-1919) She helped the Katipuneros under the leadership of Andres Bonifacio by providing them food, shelter, and other material goods. She is recognized as the Grand Woman of the revolution and the Mother of Balintawak.

List of Philippine Heroes:

1. Emilio Aguinaldo (1869 - 1964)

   first president of the Philippine Republic.

   composer of the Philippine National Anthem, "Lupang Hinirang"

10. Emilio Jacinto (1875 - 1899)

    "Brains of the Katipunan"

11. Graciano Lopez  Jaena (1856 - 1896)

    "Greatest Orator of the Propaganda Movement"
