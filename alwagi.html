10. being always lait to being always early​


Answer:

Being always late and being always early are both extreme behaviors that can be disruptive to one's personal and professional life.

If someone is always late, they may miss important appointments, cause delays in group projects, and come across as inconsiderate or unreliable. This can damage their relationships with others and harm their reputation.

On the other hand, if someone is always early, they may appear too eager or anxious, and may make others feel rushed or uncomfortable. This behavior can also be disruptive to the person's own schedule, as they may find themselves with a lot of idle time before their appointments or events begin.

Ideally, it's best to strive for punctuality - that is, arriving on time or a few minutes early to appointments and events. This shows respect for others' time and allows for a smooth and efficient flow of activities. However, it's also important to be flexible and adaptable when unexpected circumstances arise that can cause delays or early arrivals.
