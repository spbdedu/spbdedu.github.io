14. How will a car move in a rough road?


Answer:

very simple it's because of friction

Explanation:

hope it's help

The function of friction to a car moving in a rough road

Friction is a force that prevents two surfaces from moving in the same direction. Simply explained, the engine produces a force on the driving wheels that propels the car forward. The force that prevents the tyre rubber from sliding on the road surface is known as friction. However, things aren't quite so straightforward; there are two types of friction to consider: static and kinetic friction.

The frictional force between surfaces that are not moving relative to each other is known as static friction.

The frictional force between surfaces that are moving relative to each other is known as kinetic friction.

But isn't it about kinetic friction when the wheels are rolling? No. The static friction maintains the car firmly on course when driving on a dry road, regardless of vehicle speed. The contact area of the tyre does not shift relative to the road surface when examined under a microscope and in slow motion. It's merely that when the wheel rolls, new components of the tyre come into contact with the road.

15. Friction is much present on rocky or rough road TRUE ore FALSE


#BrainlyFast

TRUE

Friction is much more higher in uneven surface or much rough surface. Friction is said to be the resisting force. If you want to push object in a rough surface, you need more force to move the object because of the presence of the resisting force or friction. If you push object in a smooth surface, you need less force compared to the rough surface since friction in smooth surface is less.

Friction also produces heat like for example rubbing your hand in a few seconds. You will feel warm on your hand due to heat produced by friction.

27. Rough roads ______ the motion of a running object.​


Answer:

Rough roads slower the motion of a running object.

Explanation:

If a surface is smooth, moving objects can freely slide or run unti it because there is no hindrance in the kinetic energy the object possess. It has also lesser surface area because of being a flat surface. Bumps like those in the rough road slowers the motion of any running object because bumps cause additional surface area that needs to be passed on by the moving object.

To prevent accidents, road engineers provide bumps or even put an embossed paint to reduce the speed of the moving car.
