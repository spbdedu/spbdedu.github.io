10. Starter feed,grower feed and finisher feed 3 examples. ​


Answer:

Starter feed, grower feed, and finisher feed are three different types of animal feed that are formulated to meet the specific nutritional needs of animals at different stages of their growth and development. Here are three examples of each type of feed:

Starter feed:

1. Purina Start & Grow Medicated Chicken Feed - specifically formulated for baby chicks to provide essential nutrients and vitamins for healthy growth.

2. Nutrena NatureWise Chick Starter & Grower - contains high protein and essential amino acids to support the growth and development of young chicks.

3. Manna Pro Medicated Chick Starter Crumbles - contains added vitamins and minerals to support the immune system of baby chicks.

Grower feed:

1. Purina Flock Raiser Crumbles - designed to provide balanced nutrition to juvenile and growing chickens to support healthy muscle and bone development.

2. Nutrena Country Feeds Grower Feed - contains high levels of protein and amino acids to support the growth and development of young animals.

3. Manna Pro Organic Grower Crumbles - formulated with 100% non-GMO ingredients to support the growth and development of young animals.

Finisher feed:

1. Purina Flock Raiser Pellets - designed to provide optimal nutrition to mature chickens to support egg production and overall health.

2. Nutrena Country Feeds Egg Producer - contains high levels of calcium and phosphorus to support eggshell strength in laying hens.

3. Manna Pro Gamebird/Showbird Finisher - designed for gamebirds and showbirds to provide a balanced diet for optimal growth and performance.
