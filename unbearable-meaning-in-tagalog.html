Many bacteria are unable to grow at temperatures below 10°C and even psychrotrophic organisms grow very slowly, and sometimes with extended lag phases, when temperatures approach 0°C. 22. you are suffering from unbearable pain in the back so you need to go to the doctor. Make a conversation of you and the doctor.​


Answer:

Your doctor will examine your back and assess your ability to sit, stand, walk and lift your legs. Your doctor might also ask you to rate your pain on a scale of zero to 10 and talk to you about how well you're functioning with your pain.

These assessments help determine where the pain comes from, how much you can move before pain forces you to stop and whether you have muscle spasms. They can also help rule out more-serious causes of back pain.

29. complete resolve in the face of an unbearable obstacle​


Answer & Explanation:

Complete resolve in the face of an unbearable obstacle refers to the determination and unwavering commitment to overcome a difficult or seemingly impossible situation, despite the challenges and adversity faced.

When confronted with an obstacle that appears insurmountable, it can be easy to become overwhelmed and give up. However, individuals who display complete resolve are able to maintain their focus and determination in the face of adversity, drawing on their inner strength and resilience to keep moving forward.

This kind of resolve often requires a deep sense of purpose and a strong belief in oneself and one's ability to overcome the challenge at hand. It may also involve seeking out support from others, whether through personal relationships, professional networks, or community resources.

While complete resolve is not always easy to achieve, it can be a powerful force for positive change and personal growth, helping individuals to build resilience, develop new skills, and ultimately emerge from difficult situations stronger and more resilient than before.
