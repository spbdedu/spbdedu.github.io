Hummingbirds belong to the avian family Trochilidae, and their closest relatives are the equally fascinating swifts.

Swifts are placed in the order Apodiformes with hummingbirds.

Similarities; common swift birds are flying birds similar to humming birds. Swift, any of about 75 species of agile, fast-flying birds of the family Apodidae (sometimes Micropodidae), in the order Apodiformes, which also includes the hummingbirds.

Differences; The swift is a medium-sized aerial bird, which is a superb flyer. Sleeping, eating, bathing and even mating on the wing, swifts rarely touch the ground.
