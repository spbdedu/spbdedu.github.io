13. The area of cube balikbayan box measures(x³+3x²+3x+1) cubic,What is the measure of the sides of the balikbayan box?-step by step​


Answer:

The volume of the cube balikbayan box is given by x³+3x²+3x+1. We want to find the measure of the sides of the cube, which are all equal in length.

Let s be the length of one side of the cube. Then, the volume of the cube is s³.

We can set these two expressions equal to each other and solve for s as follows:

s³ = x³+3x²+3x+1 (substitute s³ for the volume)

s³ - x³ - 3x² - 3x - 1 = 0 (subtract x³+3x²+3x+1 from both sides)

We can recognize the left-hand side of this equation as a difference of cubes:

(s - x)(s² + xs + x²) - 3x(s + 1) - 1 = 0

We can simplify this equation by dividing both sides by (s-x) and rearranging:

s² + xs + x² - 3x - 1/(s-x) = 0

We now have a quadratic equation in s, which we can solve using the quadratic formula:

s = (-x ± sqrt(x² - 4(x² - x² - 12x - 4)))/(2)

Simplifying under the square root:

s = (-x ± sqrt(x² + 12x + 16))/2

s = (-x ± (x + 4))/2

s = -x/2 + 2 or s = -x/2 - 2

Therefore, the measure of the sides of the balikbayan box is either -x/2 + 2 or -x/2 - 2. We cannot determine which of these values is correct without additional information about the problem.

20. Sheryl received a balikbayan box from, her father who is working abroad. The box measures 90 cm long, 90 cm wide, and 60 cm high. What is the volume of the balikbayan box?​


VOLUME OF A RECTANGULAR PRISM

Problem: Sheryl received a balikbayan box from, her father who is working abroad. The box measures 90 cm long, 90 cm wide, and 60 cm high. What is the volume of the balikbayan box?

Answer: The volume of the balikbayan box is 486 000 cm³ or 486 000 cu. cm.

[tex]\Large\mathbb{SOLUTION:}[/tex]

Volume is the amount of space that a solid figure can accommodate or contain. The formula for finding the volume of a rectangular prism is V = L x W x H. Volume is expressed in cubic units like m³ and cm³.

[tex]\bold{Formula : } \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \\ \boxed{ \sf \: V = L \times W \times H \:}[/tex]

where:

V is the volume.L is the length.W is the width.H is the height.

Given values:

L = 90 cmW = 90 cmH = 60 cm

Required:

What is the volume of the balikbayan box?

Substitute the given values into the formula and solve for volume:

[tex]\sf:\implies V = L \times W \times H[/tex]

[tex]\sf:\implies V = 90 \: cm \times 90 \: cm \times 60 \: cm[/tex]

[tex]\sf:\implies V = 90 \: cm \times 5 \: 400 \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]

[tex]\sf:\implies \bold{V = \underline{ \boxed{ \bold{ \: 486 \: 000 \: {cm}^{3} \: or \: 486 \: 000 \: cu. \: cm \: }}}}\\[/tex]

Therefore, the volume of the balikbayan box is 486 000 cm³ or 486 000 cu. cm.
