The term "lovebirds" typically refers to a small species of parrot that are known for their strong pair bonding and affectionate behavior towards their mate. These birds are popular as pets because of their cute appearance and loving nature.

In a broader sense, the term "lovebirds" can also be used as a term of endearment for a couple who are deeply in love and affectionate towards each other. This usage suggests a strong emotional connection and commitment between the two individuals, similar to the bond shared by the birds.
