3. 6. A cougar with a mass of 80 kg is standing at the edge of a cliff. Find the cougar’s potential energy if the cliff is 70 m high.


Answer:

PE= 54,880

Explanation:

PE=mgh

m=80kg

g=9.8m/s² 80kg(9.8m/s²)(70m)=54,880J

h=70m

PE=54,880J
