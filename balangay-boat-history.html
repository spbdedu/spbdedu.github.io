The distinct feature of a Vinta boat is its colorful rectangular sail known as "bukay". The traditional "bukay" of the Sama-Bajau is colored in red, blue, green, yellow, and white. The sail is commonly decorated with vertical stripes and triangular shapes patterns. The simple geometrical design and patterns are influenced by Islam.

21. Balangay is a type of boat that is well-planned andcarved out of hard stone? ​


Balangay

Balangay, also spelled barangay, is a type of lashed-lug boat built by joining planks edge-to-edge using pins, dowels, and fiber lashings. They are found throughout the Philippines and were used largely as trading ships up until the colonial era. The oldest known balangay are the Butuan boats, which have been carbon-dated to 320 AD and were recovered from several sites in Butuan, Agusan del Norte.
