8. Data Table on the Movement of Animals MOVEMENT ANIMALS 1. run/walk 2 swim 3. gallop 4. hop 5. fly 6. crawl 7. waddlehelp ​


Movement of Animals

Answer:

1. run/walk - dog, cat, ostrich

2. swim - fish, dolphin, whale

3. gallop - horse, leopard, giraffe

4. hop - kangaroo, rabbit

5. fly - eagle, mosquito, butterfly

6. crawl - snake, crocodile, snail

7. waddle - duck, penguin

Explanation:

Different animals have different ways of movement. Some run, swim, gallop, hop, fly, crawl, or waddle. Their different body structures allow them to move differently. For example, birds have wings to make them fly while fishes have a streamlined body shape, fins, and tails that allow them to swim. These movements allow animals to move from one place to another. As they move, the animals are able to get food, escape from their enemies, or just merely transfer location.
