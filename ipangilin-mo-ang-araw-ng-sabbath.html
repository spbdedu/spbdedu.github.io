29. 12. Which statement is true?A. The Sabbath is the first day of the week.B. Sunday and the Sabbath are the same thing.C. The Sabbath is the seventh day of the week.​


Answer:

C. The Sabbath is the seventh day of the week.

Explanation:

It is written in the Ten Commandments which can be found in Exodus 20:8-11,

"Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy.

Six days shalt thou labour, and do all the work:

But the seventh day is the sabbath of the Lord thy God: in it, thou shalt not do any work, thou, not thy son, nor thy daughter, thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thy cattle, nor thy stranger that is within thy gates:

For in six days the Lord made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them is, and rested the seventh day: wherefore the Lord blessed the sabbath day, and hallowed it."

The Sabbath is a 4th commandment in the Ten Commandments of God given through Moses in two tables of stones in Mount Sinai during their journey when they left Egypt.

In the Portuguese Bible, we can see what day is the sabbath.

"Lembra-te do dia do sabado, para o santificar." (Exodus 20:8)

Conclusion:

Sabbath and Sunday are not the same.

The Sabbath is the seventh day of the week.
