4. mixtures can be homogenous. a sachet of 3 in 1 coffee contains coffee, sugar and powdered cream. thus, making it herterogeneous. when hot water is being poured into the said occurrence?​


Explanation:

A homogeneous mixture has components which are evenly distributed throughout the substance. A heterogenous mixture has components that are not. If the coffee is thoroughly stirred then it will be a homogeneous mixture because the cream and sugar will be evenly distributed throughout the coffee. This is why when you take a sip each sip tastes the same, if it was not evenly distributed the sips would taste differently because you would ingest different ratios of coffee, sugar and cream which would not taste very good.
