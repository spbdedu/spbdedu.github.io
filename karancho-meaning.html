3. what will happen to the kalanchoe in first day?​


Answer:

Kalanchoe plants are fun to grow from cuttings. The vegetative stems produce the best plants and root quickest. Take a 2- to 3-inch (5-7.6 cm.) section and strip off the bottom couple of leaves. Let the cutting sit out in a warm, dry location to form a callus on the end.

Los Borrachos (The Drunks) or The Triumph of Bacchus was painted by Diego Rodríguez de Silva y Velázquez on 1628-29. The painting shows Bacchus (Roman God of wine, agriculture and fertility equivalent to Greek God Dionysus) surrounded by the drunks. He is represented as a person at the center of celebration but his skin is paler to the rest for easily recognizable. It represents temporarily releasing them from their problems as Bacchus as a god who rewards or gifts men with wine.
