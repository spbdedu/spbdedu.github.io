Answer:

Being a hero is both a blessing and a curse because it comes with immense responsibility and sacrifice. Heroes are celebrated for their bravery, selflessness, and willingness to put themselves in harm's way to help others. They inspire hope, instill courage, and leave a lasting impact on society. However, being a hero also means facing constant scrutiny, criticism, and danger. Heroes often endure physical and emotional trauma, face legal or ethical dilemmas, and struggle with the weight of their own expectations. Moreover, heroes are often vulnerable to personal attacks, isolation, or even retaliation from those who oppose their actions. Thus, being a hero entails both rewards and challenges that require immense courage, resilience, and humility.

Being a hero is both a blessing and a curse because of the immense responsibilities that come with it. Being a hero can be a blessing as it brings a sense of purpose and fulfillment to one's life. On the other hand, being a hero can also be a curse as it can come with a heavy burden of expectations and scrutiny. Heroes are expected to always do the right thing, even in the face of danger or adversity. They are often under constant pressure to live up to the ideals and values they represent. In conclusion, being a hero is both a blessing and a curse. While it brings a sense of purpose and fulfillment, it also comes with a heavy burden of expectations and scrutiny. 28. anong tagalog ng curse at ng cursed​


CURSE O CURSED

Ang terminong Ingles na curse ay sumpa sa tagalog at ang cursed naman ay isinumpa na tumutukoy sa isang sitwasyon o kilos nang pagsumpa sa isang bagay.

Halimbawa ng paggamit ng salitang ito:

1.) Maraming mga lugar sa Pilipinas ang naniniwala sa sumpa.

2.) Sinasabi ng mga nakakatanda na may mga tao o bagay na isinumpa at maaaring magdulot sa mga tao ng sakuna at mapait na kapalaran.

Sumpa at Nasumpa o Sinumpa in Tagalog ng Curse at Cursed.

Halimbawa :

English : The bad witch cursed me.

Tagalog : Sinumpa ako nang masamang mangkukulang.

English : Are we already living in time of the curse ?

Tagalog : Nabubuhay ba tayo sa panahon ng sumpa?

Ano ba ang ipigsabihin ng Curse at Cursed?

Ang ipigsabihin ng Curse o cursed ay pinaparusahan ang isang tao na gamit ang kapangyarihan.
