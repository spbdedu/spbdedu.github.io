The ingredients are eggs, mayonnaise, bread, and pickle relish. On the food stand there are 3 brands for bread (10 packs July's, 15 packs Madi, 5 packs St Jose), 2 brands for mayonnaise (20 bottles Lady's Peek, 25 bottles Best Fud), 2 brands for eggs (100 pieces Farmhouse, 120 pieces Rubina) and 3 brands for pickle relish (20 bottles Lady's Peek, 25 bottles Best Fud, 30 bottles Krapt). 1. What is the probability of: a. picking Madi or St Jose for bread? b. selecting Best Fud or Kraft for the mayonnaise? c. choosing Farmhouse or Rubina for eggs? d. choosing Lady's Peek or Best Fud for pickle relish? 2. What are the odds of: a. Picking not Best Fud or Lady's Peek for mayonnaise? b. Picking Kraft or Lady's Peek for pickle relish? The cost of 1 pack of bread is Php 50.00, 1 bottle of mayonnaise is Php 100.00, a dozen of eggs is Php 90.00, and 1 bottle of pickle relish is Php 35.00. Create your own Egg Sandwich. Identify the ingredients and the number of pieces to be used in making the egg sandwich. How much will you pay for it?​


Answer:

1. a. The probability of picking Madi or St Jose for bread is (15 + 5) / (10 + 15 + 5) = 20 / 30 = 2/3.

b. The probability of selecting Best Fud or Kraft for the mayonnaise is (25 + 30) / (20 + 25) = 55 / 45 = 11/9.

c. The probability of choosing Farmhouse or Rubina for eggs is (100 + 120) / (100 + 120) = 1.

d. The probability of choosing Lady's Peek or Best Fud for pickle relish is (20 + 25) / (20 + 25 + 30) = 45 / 75 = 3/5.

2. a. The odds of picking not Best Fud or Lady's Peek for mayonnaise is (20 / 45) / (25 / 45) = 4/5.

b. The odds of picking Kraft or Lady's Peek for pickle relish is (30 / 75) / ((20 + 25) / 75) = 2/3.

To create an egg sandwich, will need:

- Bread: Madi

- Pickle relish: Lady's Peek

- Eggs: Php270.00 (3 dozens x Php90.00/dozen)
