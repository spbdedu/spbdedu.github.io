27. Mang stephen sells lechon manok. The following linear equation describes the profit P he makes: p= 30X-140 where P is in pesos. How many orders of lechon manok should Mang stephen sell in a day so he can earn a profit of P400?​


Answer:

Upang makabuo ng kita na P400, kailangan ibenta ni Mang Stephen ang X na bilang ng lechon manok sa pamamagitan ng equation na P = 30X - 140. Upang malutas ito, ikabit ang halaga ng P na 400:

400 = 30X - 140

540 = 30X

X = 18

Kaya, kailangan ibenta ni Mang Stephen ang 18 orders ng lechon manok sa isang araw upang makabuo ng kita na P400.

Step-by-step explanation:

the answer is 1/4 kg of lechon cause 2&1/2÷10 is 1/4

Question:

If Mang Simon has 10 members of his family and divided 2 and 1/2 kg of lechon that

he bought among them equally, how many kilograms of lechon will each get?

Solution:

[tex]2 \: \frac{1}{2} \div 10 \\ = \frac{5}{2} \div 10 \\ = \frac{5}{2} \times \frac{1}{10} \\ = \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} \\ = \frac{1}{4} [/tex]

Checking:

[tex] \frac{1}{4} \times 10 \\ = \frac{1}{2} \times 5 \\ = \frac{5}{2} \\ = 2 \: \frac{1}{2} [/tex]

Final Answer:

Each will get ¼ kg of lechon
