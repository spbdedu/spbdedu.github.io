30. wikang kinagisnan meaning


English

It means something you've known or been doing since you were young. It can also be what people around you (it can be your family) are doing since you were young and still remains in your memory until now.

Filipino

Nangangahulugan ito ng isang bagay na alam mo o ginagawa mula noong bata ka pa. Maaari din itong gawin ng mga tao sa paligid mo (maaari itong iyong pamilya) mula pa noong bata ka at nananatili pa rin sa iyong memorya hanggang ngayon.
