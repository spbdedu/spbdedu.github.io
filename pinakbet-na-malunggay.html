14. description of malunggay​


Malunggay

Malunggay is classified as a tropical plant that can reach a height of 9 meters. The most prized part of this tropical tree are the Malunggay pods, which contain essential oils, vitamins and nutrients. The Malunggay pods are very long, have a green to brown outer skin and contain small seeds that are winged and angled.

18. instruction of pinakbet​


Answer:

Step1

10 Min

Sauté garlic, onion, tomato, pork and bagoong in oil. Pour water and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step2

10 Min

Add kalabasa, okra and sitaw in 2-minute intervals. Add ampalaya and eggplant and simmer for 5 minutes.

Step3

20 Min

Season with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
