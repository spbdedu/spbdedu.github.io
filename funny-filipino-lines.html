21. The audience thinks 30% of Brandon's jokes are funny. What fraction of Brandon's jokes does the audience think are funny?


Answer:

The audience found [tex]\frac{3}{10}[/tex] of Bradon's jokes funny.

Step-by-step explanation:

1.) We must know that percent means out of a hundred. So by this, we can divide 30 by 100.

[tex]\frac{30}{100}[/tex]

2.) We cannot divide this as the numerator is higher than the denominator, but we do know for a fact that we can SIMPLIFY the fraction. Since the largest factor of 30 and 100 is 10, we must divide both the numerator and the denominator by 10.

[tex]\frac{30/10}{100/10} =\frac{3}{10}[/tex]

26. 1) "You don't have to be supremely funny or dynamic in the way that most describea good speaker." Explain this line.2) What makes Gandhi different from other speakers?​


A good speaker is not merely defined through his or her wit and humor.  May we not focus on these points for us to consider who is a good speaker or not.  In reality, a good speaker is beyond humor or how much he or she can entertain his or her audience.

A good speaker is someone who can confidently carry himself while humbly imparting his or her knowledge to his or her audience effectively.  His or her humor is a bonus, but a good speaker’s advocacy is for the people to learn from him or her and to create an information that will empower the existing knowledge of his or her audience.
