Meaty Spaghetti

These are the ingredients for cooking meaty spaghetti:

400 grams of spaghetti2 tablespoon oil1 piece onion5 cloves garlic1/2 kilogram ground pork2 tablespoon tomato paste500 grams of tomato sauce1/2 cup sugar1/4 teaspoon pepper1 cup of waterSalt to taste

Steps in Cooking Meaty Spaghetti

Follow the steps below in cooking meaty spaghetti:

Cook the spaghetti noodles according to package directions. Drain well and set aside.Sauté onions and garlic in the hot pan with oil until light brown.Add the ground pork and cook until brown.Add tomato paste and tomato sauce and sauté for 2 minutes.Add sugar, pepper, and water and continue to cook over medium-low heat until sauce has slightly thickened.Put salt and pepper to taste.
