12. What is the meaning of Hiatus and Furlough? Are there differences? Thanks in advance!​


Answer:

Hiatus is a pause or gap in a sequence, series, or process.

Ex. There was a brief hiatus in the war with France.

Furlough is leave of absence, especially that granted to a member of the armed services.

Ex. A civil servant home on furlough.

Explanation:

A furlough is a temporary leave of employees due to special needs of a company or employer, which may be due to economic conditions of a specific employer or in society as a whole.
