4. In what legal rights we can assert our claim over the west Philippine sea? what advice will you give president Duterte on the chinese encroachment on our island?​


AnswerIn what legal rights we can assert our claim over the west Philippine sea?

The United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea or UNCLOS. It defines the limits of a country's territorial seas, which they can explore and exploit marine resources. These territories are called Exclusive Economic Zones or EEZ. Cases such as what is happening in the West Philippines are under the jurisdiction of the International Tribunal for the Law of the Sea or ITLOS. This institution was established on 10 December 1982 as the judicial body that adjudicates disputes related to the Convention.
