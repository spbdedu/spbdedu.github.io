17. DUTIES, RESPONSIBILITIES, FUNCTIONS OF COMMITTE ON APPROPRIATION OF A BARANGAY? example committees handle by each barangay official​


Answer:

The Committee on Appropriation of a Barangay is responsible for overseeing the budget and financial matters of the barangay. Some of their specific duties, responsibilities, and functions include:

1. Review and analyze the barangay's financial status, including income and expenditures.

2. Develop and recommend an annual budget for the barangay, in accordance with the guidelines set by the Department of Budget and Management.

3. Monitor the implementation of the barangay budget, ensuring that funds are used in accordance with the approved budget.

4. Review and approve all requests for financial assistance, grants, and subsidies from individuals or organizations.

5. Conduct regular audits and financial reporting to ensure transparency and accountability in the management of barangay finances.

Each barangay official may handle different committees depending on their specific role and responsibilities. Some examples of committees that barangay officials may handle include:

1. Committee on Health - responsible for overseeing the health programs and services of the barangay.

2. Committee on Education - responsible for overseeing the educational programs and services of the barangay.

3. Committee on Environment - responsible for overseeing the environmental programs and services of the barangay.

4. Committee on Peace and Order - responsible for overseeing the peace and order situation in the barangay, including the implementation of barangay ordinances and regulations.

5. Committee on Women and Children - responsible for overseeing the welfare and protection of women and children in the barangay.

23. Anu po ba ang iba pang halimbawa ng Jocose lies, Officious lie, Pernicious lie?


Jocose lies are types of lies which are meant to be a joke. These are statements which are not true but are being told for fun. This does not intend to harm.

Example: Creating stories about your close friend and what they have done in a funny scenario.

Officious lies are also referred as White lies. These are resorted to do good and not to harm. Untruthful statement might be said but the intention is good.

Example: Telling your mom that you are okay and healthy although you are sick for her not to worry about you.

Pernicious lies are those which incurs harm and is fatal to others.

Example: Denying the accusation of stealing when in fact it was you who stole the thing owned by others.

30. what are the examples of health care officials?


Answer:

The prevailing views on the role of public health professionals refer to professionals in the academic world, without taking into account the fact that many public health professionals are government employees. For example, the American Public Health Association states that public health professionals play an active role in communicating public health information to nonscientific audiences, such as the general population or the mass media.

This ambiguity was resolved in 2002 when the American Public Health Association published a book titled Communicating Public Health Information Effectively. A Guide for Practitioners.5 The authors stated that 1 of the main activities of public health professionals is to convey public health information to different users. In the preface they noted that the book is aimed at public health professionals—those in the academic world as well as government employees—including persons in both the private and public sectors.
