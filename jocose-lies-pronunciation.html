2. What is jocose lies?


“Jocose lies” are kinds of lies that are meant to amuse or be used as a joke. In the Catholic faith, they are considered to be venial sins, which mean that committing this sin will not result in the “eternal burning” of one’s soul. However, this kind of joke is still frowned upon because, at the end of the day, it is still a lie.

12. Anu po ba ang iba pang halimbawa ng Jocose lies, Officious lie, Pernicious lie?


Jocose lies are types of lies which are meant to be a joke. These are statements which are not true but are being told for fun. This does not intend to harm.

Example: Creating stories about your close friend and what they have done in a funny scenario.

Officious lies are also referred as White lies. These are resorted to do good and not to harm. Untruthful statement might be said but the intention is good.

Example: Telling your mom that you are okay and healthy although you are sick for her not to worry about you.

Pernicious lies are those which incurs harm and is fatal to others.

Example: Denying the accusation of stealing when in fact it was you who stole the thing owned by others.

Iba Pang Uri Ng Kasinungalingan

Ang mga sumusunod ay ang iba pang uri ng kasinungalingan:

Officious Lie – Isang uri ng kasinungalingan na may layunin na pagtakpan ang isang bagay upang maiwasan ang pagkapahiya

Pernicious Lie- Isang uri ng kasinungalingan na may layunin na manira ng reputasyon ng iba at kadalasan na ito ay nakakapinsala sa iba
