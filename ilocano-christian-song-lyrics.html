MANANG BIDAY

Explanation:

Manang Biday is a traditional ilocano folksong in Northern Luzon particularly in the province of Ilocos. This song implies the courtship of a young maiden named Manang Biday. Serenading a love interest is a custom of the Filipinos. Until today, it is still practiced by the Ilocano. It is also a courtship dance. It was originally composed by Florante Aguilar.

13. How do the lyrics of the song WE OUR YOURS BY BUKAS PALAD capture what our christian faith says about our dignity as person?


Answer:

The song We Are Yours by Bukas Palad confirms and acknowledges the very truth about our own existence that we are God's creature. It accepts the fact that God made us in his own image and likeness thus He is our true master and nothing more. It also brings us to the idea that even when we were still in the wombs of our mothers, God knew us already so we are all destined to be with God from the beginning until the end of our lives.
