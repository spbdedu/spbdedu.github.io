17. How Can You Preserve Santol Fruit ?​


Answer:

How to make santol preserve

1. Select sound, ripe santol. Wash and blanch in boiling water for 10 minutes.

Peel thinly and evenly. Soak peeled santol in cold water.

2. Cut into halves and remove seeds. Soak in water for three days, changing the water everyday.

2. Wash santol and blanch in boiling water for 10 minutes. Dip in cold water and drain.

3. Boil in syrup made up of two parts sugar and one part water for 15 minutes. Let stand overnight.

How to Take Care:

The santol fruit can be pickled in salt water and can be used as a side dish together with any rice meals. The fruit skin must be peeled leaving the rind and seeds to be pickled. As for the seeds, these must not be swallowed as it caused deaths in Asia.

25. Aida, Lorna and Fe went to the farm to pick Santols. They picked 120 Santols in all. Aida picked twice as many as Lorna did, and Lorna picked 3 times as many as Fe did. How many Santols did each of them pick?​


Answer:

Fe picked 12 Santols, Lorna picked 36 Santols, and Aida picked 72 Santols.

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve, Let x be the number of Santols that Fe picked.

Since Lorna picked 3 times as many as Fe did, she picked 3x Santols.

Since Aida picked twice as many as Lorna did, she picked 2 * 3x = 6x Santols.

Altogether, the three of them picked x + 3x + 6x = 120 Santols.

This means that x + 3x + 6x = 120, or 10x = 120.

Therefore, x = 120 / 10 = 12.

Fe picked 12 Santols.

Lorna picked 3 * 12 = 36 Santols.

Aida picked 2 * 36 = 72 Santols.

Altogether, the three of them picked 12 + 36 + 72 = 120 Santols.

Therefore, Fe picked 12 Santols, Lorna picked 36 Santols, and Aida picked 72 Santols.

Let X be the number of Santols that Fe picked.

Since Lorna picked 3 times as many as Fe did, she picked 3X Santols.

And since Aida picked twice as many as Lorna did, she picked 2 * 3X = 6X Santols.

Altogether, the three of them picked X + 3X + 6X = 10X Santols.

Since they picked a total of 120 Santols, we can set up the equation: 10X = 120

Solving for X, we find that X = 12.

This means that Fe picked 12 Santols, Lorna picked 3 * 12 = 36 Santols, and Aida picked 2 * 36 = 72 Santols.

Therefore, Fe picked 12 Santols, Lorna picked 36 Santols, and Aida picked 72 Santols.
