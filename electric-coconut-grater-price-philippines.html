2. The importance of the Coconut production in the Philippines


Coconut, also known as the “tree of life”, is one of the most important crops in the Philippines. It is considered a major export, contributing 3.6% of the country's gross value-added (GVA) in agriculture, next to a banana, corn, and rice.  Generally, the coconut production had been stagnant from 2009 to 2017. There had been no significant increase =in production's growth rate. The production has started to slow down from 2010 to 2013 mainly due to the infestation of the coconut scale insects and the occurrence of two major typhoon.

To determine whether the two fractions is greater than, we can use methods such as cross-multiplication, converting fractions to decimals, or converting fractions to percentages.

Using cross-multiplication:

[tex]\tt \dfrac{a}{b}=\dfrac{c}{d}[/tex]

[tex]\tt ad=bc[/tex]

[tex]\tt \dfrac{2}{3}=\dfrac{1}{4}[/tex]

[tex]\tt 8=3[/tex]

Therefore, [tex]\tt \dfrac{2}{3}[/tex] is greater than [tex]\tt \dfrac{1}{4}[/tex].

Using converting fractions to decimals:

[tex]\tt \dfrac{2}{3}=0.667\\\\ \dfrac{1}{4}=0.25[/tex]

[tex]\tt \dfrac{2}{3}[/tex] is still greater than [tex]\tt \dfrac{1}{4}[/tex].

Using converting fractions to percentages:

[tex]\tt \dfrac{2}{3} =66.67\% \\\\\dfrac{1}{4}=25\%[/tex]

[tex]\tt \dfrac{2}{3}[/tex] is still greater than [tex]\tt \dfrac{1}{4}[/tex].

We have concluded that  [tex]\tt \dfrac{2}{3}[/tex] is greater than [tex]\tt \dfrac{1}{4}[/tex].
