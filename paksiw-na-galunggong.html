4. how can you describe a Filipino tradition paksiw ​


Answer:

Paksiw is a term associated to a dish that is cooked with vinegar and garlic. Lechon Paksiw is a Filipino pork dish made from leftover roast pig which is known as “Lechon”. Aside from using roast pig, leftover Lechon kawali can also be used. Making this dish is a practical way to recycle leftover pork.

9. mag bigay ng 5 hakbang sa pag hahanda ng paksiw na saluyot?​


INGREDIENTS:

2 handfuls of saluyot leaves

6 cloves of garlic, crushed

fish paste (bagoong)

cane vinegar

water

INSTRUCTIONS:

1.Mix half a cup of water and one tablespoon of

fish paste in a pot.

2. The mixture need not to be salty at this point. Bring to a boil over low heat.

3. Add the crushed garlic cloves. Let it simmer for a couple of minutes.

4. Add the saluyot leaves. Cover the pot and let the mixture simmer over low flame.

5.Once the leaves are half-cooked, add one to two tablespoons of cane vinegar.

6. Do not stir. Cover the pot and simmer for five to ten minutes. Stir the mixture.

7. Balance the acidity of the vinegar with the saltiness of the fish paste by adjusting the two ingredients if needed.

8. Once satisfied, continue cooking the mixture uncovered until almost dry

13. give some of description of paksiw na isda​


Answer:

Paksiw na Isda is a type of Filipino dish where fish is poached in vinegar seasoned with fish sauce and finger chillies. The name of the dish is derived from the word “paksiw” which is a cooking method where meat and seafood is simmered in vinegar hence you will see this term not only used on fish but also pork.

16. anong isda ang kumakain ng galunggong​


Answer:

Galunggong, also known as round scad or mackerel scad, is a small, abundant species of fish found in the Pacific Ocean and Southeast Asia.

Galunggong is a primary food source for many larger predatory fish, such as tuna, mackerel, and swordfish. They are also preyed on by larger pelagic species like sharks, barracuda and billfish.

whose name means "to cook and simmer in vinegar."

Ang english ng paksiw ay maaaring isalin sa "Vinegar Stew".

Paliwanag:

Ang paksiw at ang sinigang ay parehong "Stew" ang kahulugan sa english. Ngunit, isa sa pagkakaiba ng dalawang putahe na ito ay sa ginagamit nilang sangkap sa pagluluto. Ang sinigang ay gumagamit ng prutas tulad ng sampalok, kamias, kamatis, at batuan bilang pampa-asim na sangkap habang ang paksiw naman ay suka ang ginagamit.
