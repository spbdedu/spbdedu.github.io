Answer:

Description:

reduce is to use less things

reuse is to re-using an item or object

recycle is like reusing but recycling is fixing items to make it usable

repair is to fix an item

recover, reclaim, retrieve are to regain literally or figuratively something or someone.

Example:

reducing less plastic for our environment

reusing a plastic bottle for other juice or water

recycling junk to make something better

repairing shoes to make it reusable

recovering a waste to make the rivers clean
