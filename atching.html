Spring Balance - It measures the force in Newton.The answer is 'D. Spring balance' .

E. 5.) Symbol for work.

'W' - Symbol for work. Work (or 'W') is a scalar quantity. It has only magnitude and doesn't have direction.The answer is 'E. W' .

Potential Energy - Energy gained or lost by object due to its position and reference level.The answer is 'A. Potential Energy' .
