12. how my brother leon brought home his wife implication


How My Brother Leon Brought Home A Wife is a story that tells how one should treat others with respect even if they are not of the same place and do not share the same culture. In the story, it was Baldo who was expected to show courtesy towards his brother Leon's wife who is Maria. Several events in the story shows how people often treat newbies or those person that they hardly recognize since they are new in the place and they do not behave in the same manner that most people of the place behave. Similarly, the manner by which parents treat their son's/daughter's other half was very common. They need to have some sort of approval in order to gain their respect and eventually be accepted and recognized as part of the family.
