2. ano ang ibig sabihin ng live life to the fullest sa tagalog


Walang direktang katumbas ang Ingles na kasabihang "Live Life to the fullest."

Pero dahil ang kasabihang ito ay mayroon ding ibang mga kataga o kasabihan na parehas ang kahulugan, pwede natin malaman kung ano ang ibig sabihin nito sa Filipino. Tignan natin ang ilang halimbawa.

Live like there is no tomorrow.

Mabuhay ka ng parang wala nang bukas.

Enjoy life.

Masiyahan ka sa buhay.

Eat, drink, and be merry!

Kumain, uminom, at magsaya!

You only live once.

Minsan ka lamang mabubuhay.

Mula sa mga halimbawang ito, masasabi natin na ang kahulugan ng mga salita na ito ay na dapat natin pahalagahan ang buhay na mayroon tayo at dapat natin gawin ang makakaya natin upang mabuhay ng masaya, malusog, at walang pagsisisi.

5. why we must live our life to the fullest? explanation please.


Explanation:

I guess it means making good use of the hand you have been dealt in life.

It doesn’t mean you need to have what for some reason has come to be called a ‘bucket list’ of ridiculous things to do before you die. We don’t all have to go bungee jumping or swimming with great white sharks just to prove we were here.

First, be true to yourself, rather than what others try to force on you, otherwise you will never have peace of mind or a feeling of self-worth. Every day, have a period set aside when you hold yourself to account, acknowledge what you achieved, and make a modest tentative plan for the days ahead. No you don’t have to plan to be running a company worth billions, within 10 years. There are plenty of worth goals.
