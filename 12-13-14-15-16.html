6. There are 16 students who took the diagnostic test. there scores are 13, 14, 15, 15, 15, 15, 17, 12, 12, 18, 14, 14, 16, 13, 16, and 18. if fatima got a score of 15, what is her percentile rank?


HOW TO CALCULATE PERCENTILE RANK:

Step 1: List out all the given data in an increasing order

12, 12, 13, 13, 14, 14, 14, 15, 15, 15, 15, 16, 16, 17, 18, 18

Step 2: Use this formula

P = (100 (i - 0.5)) / n

Where i is the rank of the data when ordered from least to greatest and;

n is the number of operations.

Step 3: plug in the numbers

The score of 15 is 8th in the ranking of lowest to greatest score.

P = (100 (8 - 0.5)) / 16

P = (100) (7.5) / 16

P = 750 / 16

P = 46.88

This means that Fatima’s percentile rank is 46.88%. To interpret this, it means this is the percentage of the class who scored lower than Fatima is 46.88%.

Another way to visualize this is by using a visual plotting in statistics called the box and whisker plot.

To learn more about percentiles, see: https://brainly.ph/question/16663152
