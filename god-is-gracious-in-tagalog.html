18. what is four gracious plants???


The Four Gentlemanly Plants, alternately called The Four Gracious Plants, or the Four Seasons symbols, consist of plum blossoms, orchids or wild orchids, chrysanthemums, and bamboo. The Four Gracious Plants is not only the painting but also the integrations of Oriental cultures and the spiritual meanings and values.
