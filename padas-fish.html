16. Pada segitiga PQR diketahui sudut P:sudut Q:sudut R =7:5:3.besar sudut Q =.....


I noticed that the question above is Indonesian, all we have to do is to translate it into English for better understanding.

"In triangle PQR has known angle P:angle Q: angle R=7:5:3, Angle Q=..."

To answer, we should recall the ratio and proportion topic.

Let x be the common factor

Recall that the sum of angles of a triangle is 180 degrees.
So, [tex]Angle P + Angle Q+Angle R=180 degrees[/tex]
      [tex]7x+5x+3x=180[/tex]
      [tex]15x=180[/tex]
      [tex]x=12[/tex]

Then Angle P = 7x =7(12) = 84 degrees
         Angle Q = 5x = 5(12)= 60 degrees
         Angle R = 3x = 3(12) = 36 degrees

To verify the answer just simply add the 3 angles if the sum is 180.
