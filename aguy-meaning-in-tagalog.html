The idea that the speaker is a Filipino and that their blood contains the "immortal seed of heroes."The idea that this "seed" has flowered in the past through acts of courage and defiance.The idea that this "seed" is immortal and has been present in the hearts of various Filipino heroes throughout history.The idea that this "seed" has bloomed in times of frustration and resistance, as well as times of triumph and victory.The idea that the speaker's own blood contains this same "seed" and that they are a part of the same heroic tradition.
