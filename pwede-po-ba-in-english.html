3. pwede po Ba pasagot?English po10 Filipino Characteristic that we must possess​


Answer:

1. Honest

Their honesty is one of the traits that you should look forward to. It helps you feel more comfortable working with them, knowing that they are dependable and trustworthy.

2. Respectful

Filipinos have been taught to be respectful the day they learned to speak their first words. Of course, Filipino sales representatives won’t say “po” or “opo” while conversing with foreigners.

3. Calm And Collected

It doesn’t matter if you’re shouting on the phone about a defective item or raging about delayed delivery, Filipino CSRs exhibit a calm demeanor during times of escalation or pressure. Loud voices, cursing, or threats won’t faze them from doing their task rightfully.

4. Resourceful

Filipino employees become MacGyvers when situations arise and they need to improvise.

24. english pwede niyo ba po ako tulungan kung paano mai-identify ang prepositional phrases


Prepositional phrases are phrases that consist of a preposition and an object of the preposition. The object of the preposition can be found after the preposition. Prepositional phrases can function as an adjective or an adverb.

Meet me at the park near the mall. (Adjective since it describes the word park).
I'm going down the hill. (Adverb since it describes the word going).
A prepositional phrase will function as an adjective or adverb. As an adjective, the prepositional phrase will answer the question Which one?
