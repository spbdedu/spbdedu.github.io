23. 1. Why did God create these 10 beneficial laws or these 10 Commandments?‚Äã


GOD CREATED 10 COMMANDMENTS

Answer:

Why did God give us the Ten Commandments, you might wonder?

The Ten Commandments were first given by God for the personal well-being of His people. "I am the LORD your God," God said in Exodus 20:2, "who brought you out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of slavery." God had already redeemed and delivered His people from slavery. He was now telling them the rules that would govern their life now that they were out on their own. Remember this: there is never any indication that obeying the law would lead to their redemption. The idea that we may win God's approval by obeying the law is not found in the Bible. At this point, the Israelites had already been redeemed. The law was enacted to protect their personal safety.
