2. procedures in making pork embutido and the engredients ​


Answer:

ingredients

• 1 lbs. Ground Pork

• 3 pcs. Hotdog, cut in half

• 2 pcs. Eggs, hard-boiled

• ¼ cup Sweet Pickle Relish

• ¼ cup Tomato Sauce

• 1 Egg, raw

• 1 cup Cheddar Cheese

• 1/2 cup Red Bell Pepper, minced

• 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper, minced

• ¾ cup Raisins

• ½ cup Carrots, minced

• ½ cup Onion, minced

• Salt and Pepper

• 1 cup Bread Crumbs

Instructions (procedure)

1. In a Bowl, Mix all ingredient well. (Except Hotdogs and Boiled Eggs)

2. Spread the mixture on an aluminum foil.

3. Arrange the Hotdogs and hard boiled eggs on top of the mixtures.

4. Roll together with the foil into a cylindrical shape.

5. Steam for an hour.

6. Chill in the refrigerator.

7. Slice and Serve.

6. Please refer to the recipe in making “Embutido”, list down the appropriate tools and equipment to be used. Group them according to function.


Answer:

Instructions

In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients except for the water. Mix altogether until well blended.

Wrap the mixture using aluminum foil. Cut a piece of aluminum foil to 12x8 inches. Lay it flat on a table. The shiny part of the foil should face down. Scoop 1 cup of mixture and place it on the middle top part of the foil. Mold into a cylinder. Roll the foil from top to bottom. Secure both ends of the foil. Perform this step until the mixture is consumed.

Boil water in a pot. Arrange the wrapped embutido in a steamer. Steam for 1 hour.

Remove from the steamer. Let it cool down. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours. Slice into serving pieces.

Transfer to a serving plate. Share and enjoy!

1. EMBUTIDO

Make sure na ang iyong giniling na baboy ay fresh at ang kasim na part ang iyong ipagiling. Sa foil na ibalot ang mga mixture wag na sa dahon ng saging dahil lalasa ang katas ng dahon sa iyoing embutido.

make sure na inin o hindi masabaw ang iyong adobo. pakuluin mo nang pakuluain hanggang sa manuot ang lasa. lagyan mo ng laurel ata buong paminta. liver spread is optiuonal pero mas masarapa ito kapag mayroon. Generous amount of toyo, suka and sukal.

durugung maigi at pinuhin o liitin ang hiwa sa mga lahok na gulay. Mas masarap ito kung iyong aanghangan siling labyo ang magandang gamitin. Karne ng baka ang iyong ilahok, mas malasa ito. Ang kamatis ay siguraduhing nadurog bago timplahan.

Instructions

In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients except for the water. Mix altogether until well blended.

Wrap the mixture using aluminum foil. Cut a piece of aluminum foil to 12x8 inches. Lay it flat on a table. The shiny part of the foil should face down. Scoop 1 cup of mixture and place it on the middle top part of the foil. Mold into a cylinder. Roll the foil from top to bottom. Secure both ends of the foil. Perform this step until the mixture is consumed.

Boil water in a pot. Arrange the wrapped embutido in a steamer. Steam for 1 hour.

Remove from the steamer. Let it cool down. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours. Slice into serving pieces.

Transfer to a serving plate. Share and enjoy!
