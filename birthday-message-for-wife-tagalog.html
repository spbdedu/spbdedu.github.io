5. lito is 12 years older than this wife. if we combine lito's age and his wife, the sum is 60. what is the age of lito's wife?


sum of their age is 60
lito is 12 yrs older, therefore lito's wife is 48 yrs old

Let X be the age of lito's wife.
X+12 be lito's age since he is 12 yrs older than his wife.
We add these equations and equate it to 60.
X+X+12=60
Combine like terms and transpose 12 to the right.
2X=48
Divide both sides by 2
X=24
So, lito's wife is 24 yrs old.
To find lito's age we just add 12 to 24 , lito is 36 yrs old

18. Mr. Terte bought a nice dress for his wife as a birthday present and put it in a box. To make it more appealing, she planned to cover the box with a wrapper. If the box is 20 cm long , 10 cm wide, and 6 cm high, how much wrapper does he need ?​


Answer:

PILI NALANNG PO KAYO

surface area = 2lw + 2lh + 2hw  =  2(lw+lh + hw) = 2(12x8 + 12x4 + 4x8)  = 2(96+48+32) = 2(176) = 352 square inches

divide by 144 to convert to square feet  = 352/144 = 2 4/9 ft^2= 2.4 square feet, rounded to one decimal place

or

A rectangular box has 6 sides: top/bottom, front/back, left side and right side.

The amount of wrapping paper needed to cover the top and bottom is the same and it is 12 inches x 8 inches = 12•8 = 96 square inches each so combining both top and bottom, it is 96•2 = 192 sq in.

The amount of wrapping paper needed to cover the front and back is the same and it is 8 inches x 4 inches = 8•4 = 32 square inches each so combining both front and back, it is 32•2 = 64 sq in.

The amount of wrapping paper needed to cover the left and right sides is the same and it is 12 inches x 4 inches = 12•4 = 48 square inches each so combining both left and right sides, it is 48•2 = 96 sq in.

Adding these all together we get 192 + 64 + 96 = 352 square inches

It takes 352 square inches of wrapping paper to wrap a 12 x 8 x 4 inch box
