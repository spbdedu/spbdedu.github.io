13. Please can you make me a wonderful poems for our mother earth please poem for mother earth : nature not including pandemics or wala labot sa pandimic


I Wandered Lonely as a Cloud

I wandered lonely as a cloud

That floats on high o’er vales and hills,

When all at once I saw a crowd,

A host, of golden daffodils;

Beside the lake, beneath the trees,

Fluttering and dancing in the breeze.

Continuous as the stars that shine

And twinkle on the milky way,

They stretched in never-ending line

Along the margin of a bay:

Ten thousand saw I at a glance,

Tossing their heads in sprightly dance.

The waves beside them danced; but they

Out-did the sparkling waves in glee:

A poet could not but be gay,

In such a jocund company:

I gazed–and gazed–but little thought

What wealth the show to me had brought:

For oft, when on my couch I lie

In vacant or in pensive mood,

They flash upon that inward eye

Which is the bliss of solitude;

And then my heart with pleasure fills,

And dances with the daffodils.

– William Wordsworth

Answer:

Abiotic Factors are the non-living parts of the environment that can often have a major influence on living organisms.Abiotic factors include water,sunlight,air,soil and temperature

Abiotic Factors are categorized into Three:

1.)Climatic Factors-Include Sunlight, temperature,water,humidity and wind.

2.)Edaphic Factors:refers to the physical and chemical properties of soil

3.)Physiographic Factors-Refers to the physical nature of the land area like slope and Altitude

Biotic Factors refers to all living things in an ecosystem such as plants and animals.Biotic component of ecosystem are classified as producers,consumers and decomposers

•Producers(Autotroph)Are organisms that can make their own food by synthesizing sunlight,water and carbon dioxide throught the photosynthesis such as plants and algae.

•Consumers(heterotroph)Are organisms that depend on producers of other consumers for their food.Consumers are classified into three namely

•Decomposers digest complex molecules in dead organic matter into simpler inorganic compounds.They absorb the soluble nutrients as their food.Some examples are bacteria and fungi
