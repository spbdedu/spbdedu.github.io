11. Giri and bettye both collect stamps. Bettye has p stamps. Giri has 10 more than twice the number of stamps that bettye has. Together they have 400 stamps. Which of those equations can be used to find p. The number of stamps that bettye has?​


Answer:

Bettye has 130 stamps

Giri has 270 (since she has 10 more than 2 x 130)

Step-by-step explanation:

P + [10 + 2(P)] = 400

group same terms:

10 + (P + 2P) = 400

10 + 3P = 400

transfer 10 on the other side of the equation

3P = 400 - 10

divide both sides of the equation by 3

3P / 3 = 390 / 3

P=130

400 = p + (2p + 10)

400 = 3p + 10

400 - 10 = 3p

390 = 3p

p = 130

= 2p + 10

= 2(130) + 10

= 260 + 10

= 270

130 + 270 = 400

Bettye has 130 stamps.

Giri has 270 stamps.
