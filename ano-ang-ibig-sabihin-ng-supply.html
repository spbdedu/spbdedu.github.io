1. ano ang ibig sabihin ng supply?


Supply

Answer:

Ang salitang supply o suplay sa Filipino, ay isang termino na ginagamit upang ilarawan ang anumang produkto o serbisyo na maaaring ipagbili ng isang tao. Ito rin ay maaaring tumukoy sa mga resources na makikita sa ating bansa na maaaring gamitin sa paggawa ng mga produkto. Ang supply ang siyang sumusubok na mapunan ang mga demand ng isang tao o bansa.  

Mahalaga ang supply sa ekonomiks sapagkat isa ito sa mga nagtatakda sa maaaring maging takbo ng ekonomiya ng isang bansa. Ito rin ang nagiging batayan ng maaaring maging presyo ng isang produkto. Ang supply ay kaugnay ng mga salita tulad ng demand, presyo, at iba pa.

28. ano ang ibig sabihin ng demand at supply


Supply and Demand

What is supply and demand?

First let’s talk about supply. Supply talks about the amount of a good that is supplied in the current market depends not only on the price of the good but also on other potential factors, such as the production of technology, the price of the product, the cost of labor, and other factors. In analyzing supply you need to understand the relationship the price of the good and the price of the materials in creating that good.Second let’s talk about demand. Demand talks about on how much the consumer wants to acquire that certain product. The quantity of a certain product is connected to how much the consumers on a certain market wants that product. In other words, if the demand is slowly rising, so is the price of the product.

Examples of supply and demand

Jollibee foods corporation's supply of their fried chicken is lower than the demand of the consumers.

Maximo is a street vendor and he is selling some buko juice to the students. Everyday, Maximo's supply of buko juice will run out because of the overwhelming buyers.

KFC Foods Corporation's supply of their chicken is higher than the demand of the consumers.
