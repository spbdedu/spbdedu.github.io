Answer:

tago o balat ay isang hayop balat ginagamot para sa paggamit ng tao. Ang salitang "itago" ay nauugnay sa salitang Aleman na "Haut" na nangangahulugang balat. 26. How will you describe chicharon using the five sensory images?


Auditory - hearing the crunchiness when eating.
Gustatory - tasting the oil in the chicharon while eating.
Olfactory- smelling the chicharon thus making every person who smell it want to eat it.
Visual - seeing the unopened pack of chicharon
Tactile - touching the chicharon and feeling the oil in it
