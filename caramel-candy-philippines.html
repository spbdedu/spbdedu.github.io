4. Find the probability of getting coffee or caramel candies.​


Answer & Step-by-step explanation: PA BRAINLIEST

To find the probability of getting coffee or caramel candies, we need to know the total number of coffee and caramel candies, as well as the total number of candies.

Let's say there are 10 coffee candies, 20 caramel candies, and 30 candies in total.

The probability of getting a coffee candy is 10/30 or 1/3.

The probability of getting a caramel candy is 20/30 or 2/3.

To find the probability of getting either a coffee or a caramel candy, we can add the probabilities:

P(coffee or caramel) = P(coffee) + P(caramel) = 1/3 + 2/3 = 3/3 = 1

Therefore, the probability of getting either a coffee or a caramel candy is 1 or 100%.
