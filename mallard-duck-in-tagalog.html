1. What are facts about mallard ducks?


In fact, the mallard is thought to be the most abundant and wide-ranging duck on Earth. Mallards prefer calm, shallow sanctuaries, but can be found in almost any body of freshwater across Asia, Europe, and North America. They're also found in saltwater and brackish water and are commonly found in wetlands.


2. salted mallard duck eggs introduction​


Brining or curing an egg in clay produces salted egg. In most duck farms in the Philippines, clay curing is common. This procedure entails covering duck eggs in a clay-salt mixture and storing them at room temperature for 18 days.


3. how to develop mallard duck salted eggs.​


Answer:

Salted egg is produced by brining or by curing in clay. In most duck farms in the Philippines, curing in clay is often used. This method involves coating duck eggs with a mixture of clay and salt then stored indoors at room temperature for 18 days.


4. katangian ng mallard na pato​


Answer:

Ang Mallard ay isang halimbawa ng isang klasikong ligaw na pato. Maaari mong sabihin ang pamantayan. Ang hugis ng katawan ng mallards ay naka-streamline, at ang leeg ay bahagyang mas maikli kaysa sa iba pang mga species. Ang mga pakpak ng ibon ay malakas, ngunit hindi masyadong mahaba. Lamang ang uri na dapat magkaroon ng isang first-class flyer. Mahusay na manatili sa hangin si Mallard sa mahabang panahon. Ang buntot ng pato ay medyo maikli at mga taper patungo sa tip. Ang tuka ay pinahiran, nilagyan ng mga gilid na may mga espesyal na ngipin, na kung saan ay talagang isang filter (pinapayagan nila ang tubig na dumaan at bitag ang plankton).


5. what is Mrs. Mallard's lingering illness?​


Answer:

Mallard is introduced as a woman suffering from a heart condition who is unaware of her husband's recent death. Mrs. Mallard's heart condition foreshadows her heart attack, which takes place at the end of the story when she discovers that her husband is still alive.
