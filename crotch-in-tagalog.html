13. When measuring for the crotch, what are the body parts that are needed to consider and why?​


Answer:

Pull the measuring tape from the crotch inseam up to the top of the back waistband. Then, add this number to the front rise number to get the crotch length. For example, if your front rise measurement is 13 inches (33 cm) and the back rise is 15 inches (38 cm), your crotch length measurement is 28 inches (71 cm).

A French curve ruler is commonly used in drafting curves in the pattern of the arm, neckline, and crotch.  The French curve or armhole curve is the regular curved ruler used for fashion design. It's very convenient for making common pattern adjustments. A French curve ruler is made from materials like metal, wood, or plastic composed of many different divisions of the clothoid curve. The French curve ruler was invented by a German mathematician named Ludwig Burmester.

Crotch length. Measure from center front waist through crotch to center back waist. Mark the measurement from the front waist to the center of your body and the measurement from back waist to the center of your body (Figure 5).
