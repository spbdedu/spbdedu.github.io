15. How much MET will you spend while sitting at rest?A. 1 METB. 2 METsC. 3 METsD. 4 METs​


Answer:

A.1 MET

Explanation:

One MET is approximately 3.5 milliliters of oxygen consumed per kilogram (kg) of body weight per minute.So for example,if your weigh 160 pounds (72.5 kg)you consume about 254 milliliters of oxygen per minute while you're at rest (72.5 kg × 3.5 mL)
