14. 2. Mang Andoy is going to put eggs in trays of 6 eggs and 12 eggs What is thesmallest number of eggs that Mang Andoy can put using the trays?LCM 6=12=​


The smallest number of eggs that Mang Andoy can put using the trays is 12 eggs.

Make a line of your numbers. You can divide your numbers by prime numbers if both numbers can be divided evenly by that prime.

Find the LCM - Cake / Ladder

2 | 6 12

3 | 3 6

  --  --

  1   2

You are done when there are no more primes that divide evenly into both numbers.

The LCM is the sum of the numbers in the left column and bottom row of the L-shaped table. 1 doesn't matter.

LCM = 2 x 3 x 2

LCM = 12

Therefore,

LCM(6, 12) = 12
