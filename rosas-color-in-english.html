2. lesson learned in alamat ng rosas english​


The lesson learned in "Alamat ng Rosas" (Legend of the Rose) is the importance of humility and gratitude. The story shows how a proud prince's arrogance and entitlement caused him to lose everything he held dear, including his kingdom and the love of his life. However, through his humility and willingness to admit his mistakes, he was able to regain what he had lost and learn to appreciate the simple things in life, such as the beauty of a single rose. The story reminds us to be humble, grateful, and to treasure what is truly valuable in life.
