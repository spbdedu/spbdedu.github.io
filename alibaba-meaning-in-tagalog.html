15. summary of the story alibaba and the forty thieves.


Answer:

Summary of the story of Alibaba and the forty thieves

Ali Baba stumbles upon a tribe of thieves as they come and go from a magic cave, filled with gold and exotic treasures from their plunders.

Ali Baba learns the secret message (“Open Sesame”) to open and close the cave and plunders it himself when the thieves aren’t around. His theft is discovered when his greedy brother (who forced him to reveal

the location of the treasure cave) was killed by the thieves in the cave and the thieves wants revenge. It isonly through the dedicated efforts of Ali Baba’s slave girl, Morgiana, that his life is spared (and all the thieves killed) by foiling all of the thieves’ plot to kill Ali Baba.

After the death of their father, Ali Baba’s greedy brother, Cassim, marries a wealthy woman and becomes well-to-do, building on their father's business. Ali Baba marries a poor woman and settles into the trade of a woodcutter.

17. What is the conflict of the story alibaba and the forty thieves


A story will not be complete without this element - the Conflict. In order to tell a story just like "Alibaba and the Forty Thieves," a conflict must be created. It also builds the audience's interest in the storyline. A conflict can be internal and external in nature. In this story, there is a struggle of man against himself and opposing forces. In any case, the conflict sets the stage for the rest of the plot. Similarly, it makes the reader realize that they have to know the characters in the story.

Conflicts in the story of Alibaba and the Forty Thieves:

- Man against himself: It happened when Alibaba pitied himself for being a poor man while his brother had a better life. Alibaba remains poor despite working hard every day to make ends meet.

- Man against man: It took place when the thieves found out that someone is stealing their gold. The first was when Cassim greedily went to the cave to steal the gold. Unfortunately, he was caught and was killed. The next was when the thieves discovered that there is another person who knows about the gold. the thieves tried to kill Alibaba. Luckily, the servant girl, Morgiana saved Alibaba by killing the thieves and their leader.

The conflict plays a significant role in the story. However, there are other kinds of external conflicts. It can also occur because of other characters, acts of nature or society itself.

26. What is the climax of alibaba and the forty thieves?


ALIBABA AND THE FORTY THIEVES is a folk tale about Ali Baba, a woodcutter married to a poor woman who found out about the secret cave of a federation of thieves. The magic word “Open Sesame” was used to open the cave and “Close Sesame” to close it. He got a bag of gold coins because of this secret.

 

His greedy brother named Cassim was killed by the thieves when he tries to get a lot of golden treasures in the cave but was not able to get out because he forgot the magic word to get out of the cave. Ali Baba found Cassim’s body torn into pieces and he carried it home.

 

The climax happened when the federation of thieves found out that there was another culprit aside from his brother who knew the secret about the cave. They got furious to find and kill the other culprit when they found that Cassim’s dead body was gone from the cave. The plotted plans to kill Ali Baba, but because of his woman servant named Morgiana, his life was saved from the plans of assassination. To the extent that even the leader of the group of thieves went to Ali Baba’s house and pretended to be an oil merchant, while the 37 thieves left (the 2 thieves were killed because of failure to kill Ali Baba) position themselves inside the 37 oil jars brought by the leader of the thieves but because of Morgiana’s concern for her master, he poured boiling water to the jars of oil that killed the thieves inside it while the others escaped.

 

The story ended when finally in a dinner where the chief thief who pretended to be an oil merchant attended the celebration and tried to kill Ali Baba but failed again because of the loyal Morgiana’s effort. Because of this, Ali Baba blessed Morgiana to marry his son as a reward for her loyalty.
