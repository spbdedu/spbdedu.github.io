Problem:

When Yuki was in Grade 7, his height was 166.12 cm. His height increased to 1.15 cm while he was in Grade 8. His height climbed by 1.68 inches while he was in 9th grade. His height increased by the sum of 0.85 and 0.20 while he were in the tenth grade. What is his current height?

Solution:

Yuki's height was 166.12 when he was Grade 7, and we need to find his height when he was Grade 10.

166.12 + 1.15 + + 1.68 + (0.85 + 0.20) = Grade 10

166.12 + 1.15 + + 1.68 + 1.05 = Grade 10

170 cm = Grade 10

Therefore, Yuki's height when he was Grade 10 is 170 cm.

When solving routine problems, it is important to know the steps on how to solve them because we can use them in our daily lives.

What is asked in the problem?What are the given facts?

Given facts: ₱5.00 – the cost of the ice cream, 0.85 cents – money that Leah has, ₱1.15 – money that Christy has, 0.20 cents – money that Anjie has, and ₱1.68 – money that James have.

What is the operation to be used? What is the number sentence?

Subtraction

Step 3: Solve. Subtract.
